How your money is held

Every WVDC is a claim on a real dollar.

Reserved

Will it hold its value?

Every WVDC is backed one-for-one by cash and short-term U.S. treasuries, held at regulated custodians and segregated from WorldVest's own operating funds. It is designed to stay at exactly one dollar.

Verified

Who is holding my money?

Your reserves sit in segregated accounts at named third-party custodians — not with us alone. Independent accountants attest to reserves on a published schedule, and our contracts are audited and continuously monitored.

Redeemable

Can I redeem at par whenever I want?

Redeem WVDC 1:1 for U.S. dollars or swap to USDC at par, any time. No lockups, no withdrawal windows — your money is available when claims come due.
