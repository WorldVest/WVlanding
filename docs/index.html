Will it hold its value?

Yes. Every WLDVST is backed one-for-one by cash and short-term U.S. treasuries, so it's designed to stay at exactly one dollar. Reserves are attested to by an independent accountant on a published schedule.

Can I redeem at par whenever I want?

Yes. You can redeem WLDVST for U.S. dollars, or swap to USDC at par, on a same-day basis. There are no lockups and no withdrawal windows — your money is available when you need it.

Who is holding my money?

Your reserves sit in segregated accounts at regulated custodians, kept entirely separate from WorldVest's operating funds. Custody, banking, and audit are handled by named third-party partners, not by us alone.
